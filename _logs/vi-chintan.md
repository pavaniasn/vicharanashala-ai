  <h1 class="initiative-page-h">Vi-Chintan</h1>
  <p class="initiative-page-tag">Short, reflective pieces on ideas, trends, and experiences shaping our world — written entirely without AI.</p>
</div>

Vi-Chintan is a space for human thought at its most unmediated. Each piece is intentionally written without the use of AI, preserving the authenticity of voice that only comes from a person sitting with an idea long enough to have something real to say about it.

These are 500-word explorations — short enough to read in a sitting, substantial enough to stay with you. They are not meant to conclude; they are meant to provoke. The goal is not to summarise a topic but to find the question inside it.

Themes range across education, technology, culture, and the lived experience of learning — whatever is shaping our thinking at the lab at a given moment.
