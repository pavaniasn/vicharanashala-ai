Vi-Reports documents what the lab actually did — and what it found. Each report is a 2–3 page account of a process, an implementation, or a study carried out in a real educational context.

These are not abstracts or summaries. They are accounts with enough depth to be useful: what we set out to do, how we did it, what worked, what did not, and what we would do differently. They serve as both records of practice and resources for others seeking to build or adapt similar work in their own institutions.

Vi-Reports covers everything from cohort analyses and tool evaluations to programme design decisions and unexpected outcomes in the field.
