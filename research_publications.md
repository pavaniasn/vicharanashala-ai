## Instruments Developed

- **AI Literacy Benchmarking Instrument** — measures baseline and growth in AI literacy across learner cohorts
- **Weighted Quality Score (WQS) Rubric** — evaluates instructional content quality for GuruSetu
- **AI Interview Rubric** — structured evaluation rubric for Samagama/Yaksha internship assessments
