## **Gurusetu: A National Mission for Faculty Excellence**

Gurusetu is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Education, Government of India (MoE / UGC-MMTTP), with three Indian Institutes of Technology serving as nodal centers: IIT Ropar, IIT Madras, and IIT Gandhinagar. Vicharanashala is a key partner in this project, which serves as a cornerstone for faculty development under the Malaviya Mission Teacher Training Programme.

The mission reimagines ARPIT — moving beyond its passive, one-size-fits-all model — by building a high-quality, open-access content repository structured across 9 thematic pillars. Faculty development under GuruSetu is self-paced, personalized, and verified. Resources are freely available across institutions, breaking away from rigid, cohort-only training to meet educators where they are.

Active since December 2024 · Funding sanctioned: ₹1,45,00,000 (FY 2024–25 to 2026–27)

## **Capacity Building Program in Artificial Intelligence**

Funded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India (MoE / UGC-MMTTP)

We are at a crossroads where the landscape of learning is shifting beneath our feet. We live in an age where no conversation is complete without addressing Artificial Intelligence (AI), and higher education is no exception. While there is significant discourse regarding the impact of AI on education and the need to prepare students and teachers for this revolution, one question remains: with so many programs running nationwide, why choose this Faculty Development Program?

In our AI capacity-building programs, we pivot away from a "tools-first" approach. We believe that AI literacy is not merely about mastering prompts or new software; it is about learning how to think alongside a new kind of intelligence.

**The Philosophy of Our Training**

We treat AI as a pedagogical partner. This means focusing on the ethical weight of our choices, the critical evaluation of machine output, and, most importantly, maintaining a human-centered classroom. We do more than offer hands-on training with AI tools; we nudge our participants to reflect, question, and ultimately design their own unique approaches to teaching a generation that is growing up in a world where AI is fundamental to how they live, learn, and think.

**A Benchmarking Instrument Built for Indian Higher Education**

A core research output of CBPAI is a 26-item AI literacy benchmarking instrument, currently in expert validation and pilot testing. It is designed to measure faculty AI readiness across disciplines — not just STEM — with a pedagogy-first lens. There is no comparable standardised instrument currently available in the Indian higher education context, making this an original contribution to the field.

Together, GuruSetu and CBPAI have reached **3,500+ faculty** across India, with **600+ certified** — a distinction that separates structured completion from passive participation. Combined funding secured: ₹2,15,00,000.

Active since February 2025 · Funding sanctioned: ₹70,00,000 (FY 2024–25 to 2026–27)
