## Types of events we run

**Faculty Development Programs (FDPs)**
Structured programs for degree college educators — GuruSetu, CBPAI, and domain-specific cohorts. Self-paced with live support sessions.

**Workshops**
Half-day and full-day hands-on sessions on pedagogy, EdTech tools, and instructional design — open to educators and students.

**Lab Events**
Product launches, research presentations, Guru Purnima celebrations, and community gatherings for the VLED Lab community.

**Bootcamps**
Short intensive programs for students — Full Stack Development, AI literacy, and more in collaboration with partner organisations.
