<p class="product-page-tagline">Authenticity verification for human-written content — analyzing process, not just output.</p>

<div class="product-page-section">
  <h2>The Problem</h2>
  <p>As AI writing tools become more capable, traditional plagiarism detection — which compares text against a database — can no longer verify whether content was genuinely written by a human. Text alone cannot tell you who wrote it or how. Academic integrity and professional credibility now depend on a different kind of evidence.</p>
</div>

<div class="product-page-section">
  <h2>What We Built</h2>
  <p>Vi-Notes verifies human authorship by analyzing the writing process itself: typing rhythms, pause patterns, editing sequences, and pasted content detection. Combined with linguistic analysis of the final text, it generates an authenticity score and a shareable report — a verification layer that works at the process level, not just the output level.</p>
</div>
