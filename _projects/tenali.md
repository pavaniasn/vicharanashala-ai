*Where numbers lose their menace and algebra becomes a game,*
*Tenali finds the fun that makes learning feel the same.*

Tenali is built on the belief that mathematics — from basic numeracy to advanced algebra — does not have to feel like a wall. Through a uniquely engaging approach, it transforms the experience of learning numbers into something learners actually want to return to.

---

## **About**

Tenali covers the full journey from foundational numeracy through to algebra, using a game-like structure that keeps learners invested. The platform is live and free to use.
