*What 20–30 coordinators would do — run by four people and one AI.*

Samagama is an end-to-end internship management platform built to solve a structural problem in Indian higher education: access to high-quality research internship experiences has historically been gatekept by networks, coordination capacity, and institutional privilege. Yaksha is the AI layer at its core — conducting interviews, handling student queries, issuing offer letters, and managing engagement at a scale no human team could sustain.

---

## **The Challenge**

Student internships at premier institutions have long been constrained by two compounding problems. First, the coordination burden: scheduling, screening, communication, and documentation demand resources that small research labs simply do not have. Second, access inequality: students from Tier 2 and Tier 3 institutions rarely reach the door, not because they lack the ability, but because the system was never designed to include them at scale.

The question Samagama was built to answer: can every student get a high-quality internship experience at an IIT without overburdening the human resources of the institution?

---

## **The Platform**

Samagama manages the full lifecycle of a structured internship program — from registration and screening to offer letters and ongoing engagement — with minimal human intervention.

**Automated Screening and Interviews**  
Yaksha conducts AI-powered screening interviews at scale, replacing the need for human screeners at every stage. Interviews are structured, consistent, and evaluated against a purpose-built AI Interview Rubric developed by the lab.

**Offer Letter Generation**  
Once screening is complete, offer letters are generated and issued automatically, eliminating administrative delays.

**Student Engagement at Scale**  
Yaksha handles all ongoing student communication — queries, updates, clarifications — autonomously, ensuring no student is left without a response regardless of the volume of inbound messages.

**Engagement Point System**  
Student participation is tracked through a structured engagement point system, making effort visible and creating accountability throughout the internship cycle.

## **The Impact**

Samagama + Yaksha ran its first large-scale cohort under the Summership 2026 program.

| Metric | Number |
|---|---|
| Students Registered | 8,428 |
| AI-Conducted Interviews | 5,032 (zero human screeners) |
| Offer Letters Issued | 2,943 |
| Messages Handled Autonomously by Yaksha | 4,57,728 |
| Student Sessions | 18,268 (6,142 unique users) |
| Engagement Point Transactions | 47,500+ |

This was run by a 4-person core team — the equivalent of what 20–30 coordinators would typically handle.

---

## **The Vision**

Samagama exists to make structured, high-quality internship access a right rather than a privilege. By removing the coordination bottleneck through automation, any research lab — regardless of its administrative capacity — can run a program that gives students from any institution a genuine, structured experience.

The platform is designed to grow with the program: as the number of students scales, the human overhead does not.
