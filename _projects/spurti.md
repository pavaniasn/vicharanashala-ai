<p class="product-page-tagline">Gamified progress for self-regulated learning — makes effort visible, not just completion.</p>

<div class="product-page-section">
  <h2>The Problem</h2>
  <p>Most learning dashboards show students what they have completed — not what they should do next. Without a framework for consistent effort and reflection, online learners disengage mid-course or rush at the end, and instructors have no early signal to act on before it is too late.</p>
</div>

<div class="product-page-section">
  <h2>What We Built</h2>
  <p>Spurti introduces a Health Point (HP) system that assigns visible value to consistent effort, milestone completion, and self-reflection. Through LTI integration, it connects to existing course platforms without disrupting them. Instructors get analytics on engagement patterns during the course; learners see their own progress in a way that is motivating rather than just informational.</p>
</div>
