Summership is a structured, eight-week virtual internship program run by the Vicharanashala Lab, designed to answer a difficult question: can every student — regardless of which institution they attend — get access to a high-quality research internship at an IIT?

The answer, since 2026, is yes.

Summership runs end-to-end on Samagama and Yaksha, the lab's internship management platform and AI layer. Student registration, application screening, AI-conducted interviews, offer letter issuance, and ongoing engagement are all handled autonomously. This means a 4-person core team can run what would otherwise require 20–30 coordinators — without sacrificing quality, structure, or student experience.

The program is explicitly designed to be inclusive. Tier 2 and Tier 3 institution students — who are routinely locked out of research exposure by network gatekeeping and coordination barriers — are equally eligible. The application process is standardised and the screening is AI-conducted, removing the informal filters that typically disadvantage first-generation and non-metro students.
